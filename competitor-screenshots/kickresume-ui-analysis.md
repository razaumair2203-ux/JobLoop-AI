# Kickresume UI/UX Exhaustive Research

## 1. RESUME EDITOR (Main Split-Panel Editor)

### Layout Structure -- THREE-column
- **Left panel:** Section navigator + AI tools (Proofreading, AI Writer)
- **Center panel:** Active editing area (form fields, AI rewrite buttons)
- **Right panel:** Live preview, real-time updates

### Key Features
- Drag-and-drop section reordering
- Per-section "AI Writer > Rewrite Section" button
- "Examples & Guides" button toggles preview to career center
- Help icons in every feature/section
- Customization: line spacing, font sizes, heading caps, colors, layouts

### Design Wins
- Real-time preview eliminates edit-save-preview loops
- Per-section AI granularity gives precise control

### Design Failures
- Three-column feels CROWDED on smaller screens
- Premium feature traps: "free" templates silently use Premium fonts
- Rigid sections -- can't rename "Profile" to "Summary"
- Free tier caps at 2 entries per section
- Too many options at once

---

## 2. TEMPLATE GALLERY

- Grid of full-page thumbnail previews
- Categories: Creative / Professional / Simple
- 40+ templates (44 Premium), only 4 free
- Free/Premium indicated by lock icon
- **Typography is THE differentiator** -- professional typographer curated all fonts, line heights, spacing

### Template Philosophy
- **Creative**: Bold contrasting colors, decorative, personality-forward
- **Professional**: Structured, experience-focused, high readability
- **Simple**: Minimal, negative space, max clarity, best for ATS

---

## 3. AI INTEGRATION (Deep)

### Where AI Surfaces
- In-editor per-section "Rewrite Section"
- Standalone AI Resume Writer (name + job title -> full resume)
- AI Resume Rewriter (upload PDF -> AI rewrites)
- AI Resume Checker (completeness, space usage, overused expressions)
- AI Cover Letter Writer (job title + job posting)
- AI Resume Tailoring (paste JD -> AI rewrites to match)
- AI Career Map (visual career path explorer -- novel)
- AI Career Coach (GPT-4.1 conversational)
- AI Interview Questions Generator

### Design Wins
- AI woven into editing flow, not bolted on
- Non-fabrication promise builds trust
- Multiple entry points cover all scenarios

### Design Failures
- ALL AI is Premium-only
- Output can feel generic
- Scattered across interface -- overwhelming

- Clean sans-serif for editor
- Resume templates: curated professional typeface combinations

---

## STEAL THESE
1. **Typography quality** -- typographer involvement, excellent font pairing
2. Template category taxonomy (Creative/Professional/Simple)
3. Resume-to-cover-letter template matching
4. Per-section AI rewrite controls
5. LinkedIn import as first onboarding step
6. Split-screen live preview (table stakes)

## EXPLOIT THESE
1. Three-column crowding
2. Feature overload everywhere
3. Premium bait-and-switch on fonts
4. Rigid section structure
5. Generic AI output
6. Limited free tier (nearly unusable)
7. No document organization (no folders/tags)
8. Anemic website builder
